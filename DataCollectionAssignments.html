Difference between downloading data from Google Trends and using gtrends to collect data

Downloading data from Google Trends requires us to click different download buttons that are present in the Google Trends website. This may seem like an easy task, but can get cumbersome. For example, if we are to download data from different countries across the world, we have to manually select different countries from a dropdown, and then download their data. With gtrends, this is completed with a single code. Therefore, this process is much less time consuming.
